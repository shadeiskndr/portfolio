I'm rebuilding this site to be more <Highlight>personal</Highlight> than portfolio.

The old version was a CV in HTML — a list of jobs, certificates, and projects with no real voice. Useful, but not mine. This one is supposed to feel more like a notebook: half-formed thoughts, things I learned today, books I'm working through, projects I haven't finished.

## Why now

A few reasons converged:

- I miss long-form writing. Twitter rewards hot takes, LinkedIn rewards corporate prose, and neither one is what I want.
- I have opinions about UI craft that don't fit on a CV.
- I've been reading more and want a place to keep track of it.

## What's here

The `/thoughts` section (this one) is for essays — anything longer than a few paragraphs. `/til` is for one-paragraph "today I learned" notes. `/readings` is what I'm working through. The rest is still being built.

If you're reading this, hi. There's not much yet. Come back later.
